OUR JOURNEY

Across the Miles

For years, we were just two friends separated by the distance between Indonesia and Dubai. We talked every single day, comfortably sharing our heaviest burdens, our dreams, and our most random stories.

Against the Odds

In early 2024, we realized friendship simply wasn't enough anymore. We started dating and faced the LDR head-on. We cried, we laughed, and through it all, we built a bond that no distance could ever break.

The Perfect Balance

Being the same age and both the firstborns in our families, we were so used to standing strong alone. Together, we finally found a place to let our guards down. We became each other's ultimate support system, filling the voids we had carried for so long.

Our Next Chapter

On August 15, 2026, we will finally take our vows as lifelong partners. From the friends who shared everything to soulmates sharing a lifetime, we promise to be each other's constant. Through joy and sorrow, in sickness and in health, and no matter what circumstances life may bring, we will always stand by each other's side.
